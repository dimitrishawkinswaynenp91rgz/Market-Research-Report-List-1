<p><p>Electronic Expansion Valves (EEVs) are crucial components in refrigeration and air conditioning systems. There are two main types of EEVs on the market. Electromagnetic EEVs operate using magnetic fields to control the flow of refrigerant, providing precise regulation and energy efficiency. Electric EEVs, on the other hand, use electrical signals to adjust the valve opening, offering similar benefits. Both types of EEVs offer advanced technology for optimal system performance and are widely used in various industries for their accuracy and reliability.</p></p>
