<p><p>The Hotel Central Reservations Systems (CRS) market can be categorized into two main types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises. Cloud-Based CRS refers to a system where the software and infrastructure are hosted on remote servers, accessible via the internet. This allows hotels to store and manage their data on a remote server, increasing flexibility and scalability. On the other hand, On-Premises CRS involves the installation and maintenance of the software and infrastructure on-site, within the hotel's premises. This provides hotels with complete control over their data but requires more resources for maintenance and upgrades.</p></p>
