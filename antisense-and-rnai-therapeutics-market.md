<p><p>Antisense and RNAi therapeutics are two innovative approaches in the field of molecular genetics that aim to modulate gene expression and protein production. These therapies involve using small nucleic acid sequences, known as antisense oligonucleotides (ASOs) or small interfering RNAs (siRNAs), to target and inhibit specific RNA molecules.</p><p>ASOs bind to complementary RNA sequences and prevent their translation into proteins, while siRNAs induce the degradation of targeted RNA molecules. Both approaches offer potential therapeutic applications in various diseases including cancer, genetic disorders, and viral infections.</p><p>The Antisense and RNAi Therapeutics Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
