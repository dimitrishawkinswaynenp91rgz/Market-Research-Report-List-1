<p><p>Anti-fog coatings like Foggy Guard Coating (FGC) and Defog Coating (DFC) are designed to prevent fogging on various surfaces such as eyewear, mirrors, and car windows. FGC creates a thin film on the surface that repels moisture, while DFC works by absorbing and dispersing water molecules to maintain visibility. Both coatings are effective solutions for preventing fogging in humid or cold conditions, improving safety and clarity for users in a variety of settings.</p></p>
