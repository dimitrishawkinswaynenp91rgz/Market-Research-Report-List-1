<p><p>Wheeled harvesters are commonly categorized into two main types: towed and self-propelled. Towed harvesters are typically pulled by a separate vehicle or tractor, while self-propelled harvesters have their own built-in engine for independent operation. Towed harvesters are generally more cost-effective and versatile, as they can be easily attached to different vehicles. Self-propelled harvesters, on the other hand, offer greater mobility and efficiency in harvesting operations, as they can navigate through fields and forests with greater ease and speed.</p></p>
