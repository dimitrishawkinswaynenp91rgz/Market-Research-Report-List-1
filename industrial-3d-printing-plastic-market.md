<p><p>Industrial 3D printing plastic market types include powder, filament, and liquid. Powder-based 3D printing involves layering powdered material and fusing it together with heat or chemicals. Filament-based 3D printing uses a continuous strand of plastic material melted and extruded through a nozzle. Liquid-based 3D printing utilizes liquid resin that is solidified layer by layer through curing with UV light. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in various industries, making them essential components of the industrial 3D printing market.</p></p>
