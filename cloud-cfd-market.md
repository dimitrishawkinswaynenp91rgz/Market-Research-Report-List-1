<p><ul><li>Steady Simulation</li><li>Unsteady Simulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cloud CFD market consists of two main types: steady simulation and unsteady simulation. Steady simulation involves predicting fluid flow and heat transfer under constant conditions, making it suitable for applications where the flow regime remains unchanged. On the other hand, unsteady simulation deals with time-dependent and dynamic fluid flow conditions, making it ideal for analyzing scenarios with changing flow patterns. Both types benefit from cloud computing, which allows for faster and scalable simulations, enabling efficient and accurate analysis for various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and energy.</p></p>
