<p><p>American Football Gloves market is divided into two main types - Half Finger and Full Finger gloves. Half Finger gloves are designed with exposed fingertips, providing better grip and dexterity while still offering protection to the palm and back of the hand. Full Finger gloves, on the other hand, cover the entire hand providing maximum protection against impact and abrasions. Both types cater to different preferences and playing styles, with Half Finger gloves being more popular among skilled positions while Full Finger gloves are favored by linemen.</p></p>
