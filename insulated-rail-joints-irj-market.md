<p><strong>The Insulated Rail Joints (IRJ) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Supported IRJs</li><li>Suspended IRJs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Insulated rail joints (IRJs) are used to prevent electrical connections between adjacent rails in railway tracks. There are two main types of IRJs: supported IRJs, which are fixed directly to the rails using insulation materials, and suspended IRJs, which are suspended above the track using insulating hangers. Supported IRJs are typically used in urban areas with heavy traffic, while suspended IRJs are more common in rural areas or areas prone to flooding. Both types of IRJs play a crucial role in maintaining the safety and efficiency of railway systems.</p></p>
