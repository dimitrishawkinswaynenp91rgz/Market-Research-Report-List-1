<p><p>Thin Film Evaporators (TFE) are used for the separation of volatile substances from non-volatile materials in various industries. There are two main types of TFE market - Vertical Thin Film Evaporators and Horizontal Thin Film Evaporators. Vertical TFEs are suitable for processing heat-sensitive materials and viscous liquids due to their compact design and efficient heat transfer. Horizontal TFEs, on the other hand, are ideal for continuous operation and large-scale production. Both types offer high efficiency in evaporation processes and are widely used in the chemical, pharmaceutical, and food industries.</p></p>
