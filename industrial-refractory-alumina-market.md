<p><p>Industrial refractory alumina market is segmented into four types: Calcined Alumina, Fused Alumina, Tabular Alumina, and Others. Calcined Alumina is produced through the calcination of aluminum hydroxide, while Fused Alumina is created through the fusion of bauxite in an electric arc furnace. Tabular Alumina is manufactured from high-purity alumina and has a high-tensile strength. The "Others" segment includes specialty alumina products with unique properties tailored for specific industrial applications. These diverse alumina types cater to various industries like ceramics, steel, and aerospace.</p></p>
