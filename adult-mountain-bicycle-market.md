<p><p>The adult mountain bicycle market can be categorized into four main types: Rigid, Hardtail, Softail, and Full Suspension. Rigid bikes have no suspension, making them lightweight and efficient on smooth terrain. Hardtail bikes have front suspension only, providing some shock absorption for rough trails. Softail bikes have rear suspension but no pivot point at the seat stay junction. Full Suspension bikes have both front and rear suspension, offering the best shock absorption and comfort for aggressive off-road riding.</p></p>
