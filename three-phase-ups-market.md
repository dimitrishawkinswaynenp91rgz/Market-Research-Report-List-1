<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Three-phase UPS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Offline/standby Three-phase UPS</li><li>Line-interactive Three-phase UPS</li><li>Online/double-conversion Three-phase UPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offline/standby Three-phase UPS are designed for applications with non-critical loads, providing basic power protection by switching to battery power when utility power fails. Line-interactive Three-phase UPS offer a higher level of protection by regulating voltage fluctuations without constantly switching to battery power. Online/double-conversion Three-phase UPS provide the most advanced level of protection, continuously converting AC power to DC power and then back to AC power, ensuring a stable and clean power supply at all times.</p></p>
