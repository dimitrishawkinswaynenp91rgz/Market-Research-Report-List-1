<p><p>HR software solutions can be classified into two market types: On-Premises and Cloud-based. On-Premises solutions require the software to be installed and managed on the company's own servers and infrastructure, providing greater control but requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance. On the other hand, Cloud-based solutions are hosted by a third-party provider and accessed over the internet. These solutions offer flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs, as they eliminate the need for extensive hardware and maintenance expenses. Cloud-based solutions also allow for easy remote access and integration with other software.</p></p>
