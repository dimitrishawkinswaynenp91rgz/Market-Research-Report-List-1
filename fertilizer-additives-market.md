<p><p>Fertilizer additives are categorized into different types based on their composition and chemical structure. These types include Urea, Ammonium Nitrate, Ammonium Phosphate, Ammonium Sulfate, and Others. Urea is a nitrogen-rich additive, Ammonium Nitrate provides both nitrogen and ammonium, Ammonium Phosphate contains phosphorous, Ammonium Sulfate is a source of sulfur, and Others include a variety of different compounds that provide various nutrients to plants. Each type of fertilizer additive plays a crucial role in supporting plant growth and improving crop yield.</p></p>
