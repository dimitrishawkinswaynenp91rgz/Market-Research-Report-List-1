<p><p>The temperature transmitter market consists of various types like Resistance Temperature Detector (RTD), Thermocouple, Universal, and Multichannel transmitters. RTD transmitters use the electrical resistance of metals to measure temperature, while thermocouple transmitters rely on the voltage generated by the junction of two different metals. Universal transmitters can be used with both RTDs and thermocouples, providing flexibility. Multichannel transmitters allow the simultaneous measurement of multiple temperature points. Similarly, the humidity transmitter market includes various types based on different sensing technologies. These transmitters are used in various industries to accurately measure temperature and humidity levels for process control and monitoring purposes.</p></p>
