<p><strong>The Ceramic Metal Halide Ballast Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Ended</li><li>Single Ended</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic Metal Halide Ballasts are divided into two types: Double Ended and Single Ended. Double Ended ballasts have terminals on both ends of the lamp, providing a more even distribution of light compared to Single Ended ballasts, which have one terminal. Double Ended ballasts are commonly used in professional horticulture applications, while Single Ended ballasts are popular for general lighting purposes. Both types offer efficient and reliable operation for ceramic metal halide lamps in various settings, catering to different lighting requirements.</p></p>
