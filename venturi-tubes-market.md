<p><strong>The Venturi Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Built-in Venturi Tube</li><li>Plug-in Venturi Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Built-in Venturi Tubes are integrated into the system or device they are measuring flow rates for, such as in industrial applications or HVAC systems. They are permanently installed and provide accurate measurements without the need for frequent recalibration. </p><p>Plug-in Venturi Tubes are separate devices that can be easily inserted or removed from a system for flow rate measurement. They are commonly used in testing and calibration processes where portability and versatility are required. Plug-in Venturi Tubes offer flexibility and convenience in flow rate measurement applications.</p></p>
