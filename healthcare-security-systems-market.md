<p><p>Healthcare security systems can be classified into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises security systems are installed and managed within the healthcare facility itself. They provide secure access control, video surveillance, and alarm systems, enabling healthcare organizations to have direct control over their security infrastructure. On the other hand, cloud-based security systems are hosted remotely and accessed through the internet. They offer scalability, flexibility, and remote management, allowing healthcare providers to outsource their security needs to a specialized service provider.</p></p>
