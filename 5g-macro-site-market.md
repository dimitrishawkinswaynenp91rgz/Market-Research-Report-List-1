<p><p>The 5G Macro Site market encompasses two types: SA (Stand Alone) and NSA (Non-Stand Alone). SA refers to a fully independent 5G network that does not rely on existing 4G infrastructure. This type requires dedicated resources and enables the full capabilities of 5G. On the other hand, NSA allows for an initial deployment of 5G by utilizing the current 4G network resources. Although this type offers enhanced speeds and capacity, it is not as advanced as SA as it lacks certain features exclusive to standalone networks.</p></p>
