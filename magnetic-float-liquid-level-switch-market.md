<p><p>Magnetic float liquid level switches come in two main types - top-mounted and side-mounted. Top-mounted switches are installed at the top of the tank, detecting the liquid level as it rises or falls. Side-mounted switches are installed on the side of the tank, providing accurate level detection without the need for tank penetration. Both types are widely used in industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing to efficiently monitor and control liquid levels in tanks.</p></p>
