<p><p>The Angiography Device market consists of three main types: X-Ray Angiography Devices, CT Angiography Devices, and MR Angiography Devices. X-Ray Angiography Devices use X-rays to visualize blood vessels and diagnose various conditions. CT Angiography Devices combine X-ray technology with computerized imaging to provide detailed 3D views of blood vessels. MR Angiography Devices utilize magnetic resonance imaging to create detailed images of blood vessels without the need for X-rays. These devices play a significant role in diagnosing cardiovascular diseases and guiding interventions for improved patient care.</p></p>
