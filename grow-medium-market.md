<p><p>Grow medium market types include soilless mixes, composts, gravel, inert mediums, and other specialized mediums. Soilless mixes are made from materials like peat moss, perlite, and vermiculite, providing excellent aeration and drainage. Composts consist of organic matter that enhances soil fertility. Gravel is used in hydroponic systems for stability and support. Inert mediums like sand or clay pellets do not contain nutrients but provide structure for root growth. Other specialized mediums cater to specific plant needs, such as coco coir for moisture retention.</p></p>
