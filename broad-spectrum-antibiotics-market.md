Additionally, the rising incidence of antibiotic resistance among bacteria is further boosting the demand for broad-spectrum antibiotics as they are effective against multiple strains of bacteria.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing geriatric population, who are more susceptible to infections, and the increasing number of surgeries and hospital-acquired infections are contributing to the market growth.
