<p><p>The electronic PI film market can be classified into three types based on film thickness: below 10μm, 10-20μm, and above 20μm. PI films with thickness below 10μm are used for applications where flexibility and thinness are crucial, such as flexible displays and flexible printed circuit boards. Films in the 10-20μm range find applications in electric insulation, while films with thickness above 20μm are commonly utilized in printed circuit boards and various electronic devices that require high mechanical strength and heat resistance.</p></p>
