<p><p>Automotive Ceramics Market consists of two main types: Structural Ceramics and Functional Ceramics. Structural Ceramics are used for their high strength and durability, commonly found in engine components such as pistons and valves. Functional Ceramics, on the other hand, are utilized for their unique properties such as electrical conductivity or thermal insulation. These are often found in sensors, catalytic converters, and exhaust systems. Both types of ceramics play essential roles in improving the performance and efficiency of vehicles in the automotive industry.</p></p>
