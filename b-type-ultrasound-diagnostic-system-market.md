<p><strong>The B-type Ultrasound Diagnostic System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fanshaped Scanning</li><li>Linear Scanning</li><li>Mixed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>B-type ultrasound diagnostic systems have three main market types: fanshaped scanning, linear scanning, and mixed type. Fanshaped scanning systems utilize a transducer that emits sound waves in a fan-like shape to capture images. Linear scanning systems use a transducer that emits sound waves in a linear pattern for detailed imaging. Mixed type systems combine elements of both fanshaped and linear scanning for optimal image quality. Each market type offers unique capabilities and benefits for different diagnostic needs.</p></p>
