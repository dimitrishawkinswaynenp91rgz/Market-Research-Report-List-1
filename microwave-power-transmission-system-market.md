<p><p>Microwave Power Transmission System (MPTS) is a method of transmitting electrical energy wirelessly using microwaves. There are two main types of MPTS: Induction and Magnetic Resonance. </p><p>Induction MPTS involves transferring power through the use of coils, where one coil generates a magnetic field which induces a current in another nearby coil. This is similar to how transformers work.</p><p>On the other hand, Magnetic Resonance MPTS utilizes resonant circuits to transfer power wirelessly. It involves creating resonance between the transmitting and receiving circuits, allowing efficient power transfer over larger distances without the need for precise alignment.</p></p>
