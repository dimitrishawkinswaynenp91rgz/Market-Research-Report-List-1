<p><p>Semiconductor packaging electroplating solutions are used in various applications such as copper pillar bumping, redistribution layer formation, through silicon via creation, and other packaging processes. Copper pillar bumping involves creating high-density interconnects on semiconductor devices. Redistribution layer formation helps in routing signals to different parts of the semiconductor package. Through silicon via technology enables vertical connections between different layers of a semiconductor device. Other market applications include bond pad plating, wafer-level packaging, and microbumping for increased package density and performance.</p></p>
