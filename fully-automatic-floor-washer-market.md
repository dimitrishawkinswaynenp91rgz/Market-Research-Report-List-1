<p><strong>The Fully Automatic Floor Washer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push Type</li><li>Driving Type</li><li>Back Stand Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fully automatic floor washer market consists of three main types: push type, driving type, and back stand type. </p><p>1. Push type floor washers are manually pushed by the operator and typically used for small to medium-sized areas. They are lightweight and easy to maneuver, making them suitable for narrow spaces and corridors.</p><p>2. Driving type floor washers are equipped with a drivable platform for the operator to stand on. These machines are designed for larger areas and offer enhanced productivity and efficiency. The operator can control the direction and speed of the machine, allowing for faster and more effective cleaning.</p><p>3. Back stand type floor washers are operated from a standing position behind the machine. These washers are commonly used in commercial settings, cleaning large areas quickly and effortlessly. They offer excellent visibility and control, ensuring thorough cleaning with minimal effort.</p><p>Overall, these fully automatic floor washers cater to different cleaning requirements depending on the size and nature of the area to be cleaned.</p></p>
