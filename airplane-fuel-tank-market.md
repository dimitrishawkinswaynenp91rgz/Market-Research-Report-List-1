<p><p>The airplane fuel tank market consists of two main types: flexible tanks and rigid tanks. Flexible tanks are made of durable materials that allow them to conform to various shapes, making them suitable for smaller aircraft or spaces. Rigid tanks, on the other hand, are made of sturdy materials such as aluminum or stainless steel, providing more stability and protection during flights. Both types serve the same purpose of storing and supplying fuel to the aircraft, but they cater to different needs based on the aircraft size and design.</p></p>
