<p><p>Electric cable hoists are classified into two main types based on their lifting capacity: light capacity and heavy capacity. Light capacity cable hoists are designed for lifting smaller loads, typically up to a few hundred pounds. They are commonly used in industries such as construction, automotive, and manufacturing. On the other hand, heavy capacity cable hoists are built for lifting heavier loads, often several tons. These are utilized in industries like mining, shipbuilding, and heavy equipment manufacturing. Each type caters to different lifting requirements and applications.</p></p>
