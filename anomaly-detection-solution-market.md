<p><p>Anomaly Detection Solution Market comprises two main types, namely Network Behavior Anomaly Detection and User Behavior Anomaly Detection. Network Behavior Anomaly Detection involves monitoring and analyzing network traffic patterns to identify any abnormal activities that may indicate potential threats or security breaches. On the other hand, User Behavior Anomaly Detection focuses on observing individual users' actions, such as their access patterns or system interactions, to detect any deviations from normal behavior that might indicate unauthorized or malicious activities. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing security measures and preventing potential risks in various domains.</p></p>
