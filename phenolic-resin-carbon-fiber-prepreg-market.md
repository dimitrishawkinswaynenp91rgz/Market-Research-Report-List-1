<p><p>Phenolic resin carbon fiber prepreg is a type of composite material used in various industries. There are two main types of markets for this product: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset prepreg materials are cured through a chemical reaction, which gives them high heat and chemical resistance. Thermoplastic prepregs, on the other hand, can be melted and reformed multiple times, making them more versatile in terms of processing and recycling. Both types offer unique properties and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the end application.</p></p>
