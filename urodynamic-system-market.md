<p><p>The urodynamic system is a medical device used to diagnose and evaluate various urinary dysfunctions. It measures and records the functioning of the lower urinary tract, including bladder pressure, urine flow rate, and the electrical activity of the bladder muscles. Urodynamic tests are commonly performed to diagnose conditions such as urinary incontinence, overactive bladder, and urinary retention.</p><p>The urodynamic system market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. <p><ul><li>Wireless Urodynamics System</li><li>Wired Urodynamics System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Urodynamic System Market consists of two main types: the Wireless Urodynamics System and the Wired Urodynamics System. The Wireless Urodynamics System utilizes wireless technology to transmit data from the patient to the monitor, providing flexibility and convenience. It eliminates the need for cumbersome wires and allows for easier movement during the procedure. On the other hand, the Wired Urodynamics System requires physical connections between the patient and the monitor using wires, ensuring reliable data transmission but limiting mobility. Both systems serve the purpose of measuring and diagnosing urinary dysfunctions.</p></p>
