<p><strong>The RFID for Industrial Applications Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency (LF)</li><li>High Frequency (HF)</li><li>Ultra High Frequency (UHF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID technology is commonly used in industrial applications to track and manage assets. The market is divided into three types based on frequency: Low Frequency (LF), High Frequency (HF), and Ultra High Frequency (UHF). LF RFID is typically used for access control and animal tracking due to its short read range. HF RFID is often utilized for payment systems and ticketing. UHF RFID has a longer read range and is commonly used for inventory management and supply chain tracking in industrial settings.</p></p>
