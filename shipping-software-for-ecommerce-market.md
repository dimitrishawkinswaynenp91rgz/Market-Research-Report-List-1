<p><p>Shipping software for ecommerce comes in two market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based software is hosted on the internet, allowing users to access it from anywhere with an internet connection. It offers flexibility, scalability, and eliminates the need for hardware installation and maintenance. On the other hand, on-premise software is installed and operated on the user's own servers or computers. It provides greater control and security, but requires hardware installation and ongoing maintenance. Both options have their own benefits and depend on the specific needs and preferences of businesses.</p></p>
