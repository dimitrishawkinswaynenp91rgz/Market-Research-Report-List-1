<p><p>A protective relay is an electrical device that detects abnormal conditions in an electrical system and initiates appropriate actions to protect it from potential damage or hazardous situations. It acts as a control mechanism that continuously monitors the system parameters like voltage, current, frequency, and temperature, and triggers a response when certain preset conditions are met. The primary function of protective relays is to isolate the faulty section or equipment from the rest of the system to prevent further damage and ensure safety.</p><p>The protective relay market is witnessing significant growth and is expected to continue its upward trajectory in the coming years.
