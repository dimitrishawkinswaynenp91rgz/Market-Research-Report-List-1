<p><p>Roofing software market encompasses two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise software is installed and hosted on the user's local servers, allowing for greater control over data and customization options, but requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance. On the other hand, Cloud-Based software is accessed through the internet and hosted on remote servers. It offers flexibility, scalability, and easy integration, with lower upfront costs and effortless updates. Users can access their data from anywhere at any time, making it a popular choice for many roofing businesses.</p></p>
