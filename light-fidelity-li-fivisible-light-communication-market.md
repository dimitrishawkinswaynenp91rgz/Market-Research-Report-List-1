<p><p>Light Fidelity (Li-Fi)/Visible Light Communication market encompasses three main types of products – LED lights, photodetectors, and microcontrollers (MCU). LED lights act as the source of visible light signals, while photodetectors receive and convert these signals into data. Microcontrollers (MCU) serve as the brain of the Li-Fi system, processing and transmitting the data received from the photodetectors. These three components work together to enable high-speed data transmission through visible light, offering a promising alternative to traditional Wi-Fi technology.</p></p>
