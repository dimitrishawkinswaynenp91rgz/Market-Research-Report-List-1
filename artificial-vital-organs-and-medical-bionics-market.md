<p><p>Artificial Vital Organs (AVOs) are artificially created organs that mimic the functioning of natural organs in the human body. On the other hand, Medical Bionics refers to the integration of technology with the human body to enhance or replace impaired physiological functions. This includes devices like cochlear implants for hearing impairment or pacemakers for regulating heart rhythms. Both AVOs and Medical Bionics aim to improve the quality of life and health outcomes for individuals.</p></p>
