<p><strong>The Red Vacuum Blood Collection Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Common Serum Tube</li><li>Rapid Serum Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The red vacuum blood collection tube market includes two main types: common serum tubes and rapid serum tubes. Common serum tubes are used to collect blood samples for various tests and analyses. These tubes contain a clot activator, which helps in the separation of serum from the blood cells. On the other hand, rapid serum tubes are designed to expedite the separation process by incorporating a gel separator. This gel forms a barrier between the serum and the clot, allowing for faster and easier sample analysis. Both types of tubes are widely used in medical and diagnostic settings.</p></p>
