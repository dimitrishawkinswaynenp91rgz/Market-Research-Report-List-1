<p><p>Telemedicine stethoscope market refers to the industry that provides stethoscopes for use in remote healthcare consultations. There are two types of stethoscopes in this market. Wireless stethoscopes are equipped with Bluetooth technology, allowing the transmission of audio signals to a receiver for analysis and diagnosis from remote locations. On the other hand, wire stethoscopes rely on physical connections to transmit audio signals. Both types aim to provide accurate auscultation of patients' body sounds, enabling healthcare professionals to remotely assess their condition and provide appropriate medical guidance or treatment.</p></p>
