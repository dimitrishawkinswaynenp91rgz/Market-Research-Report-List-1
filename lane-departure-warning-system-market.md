<p><p>The Lane Departure Warning System market consists of three types: Lane Departure Warning (LWD), Lane Keeping System (LKS), and Lane Centering Assist (LCA). LWD alerts the driver when the vehicle unintentionally leaves its lane, while LKS actively helps steer the vehicle back into the lane. LCA takes the assistance a step further by continuously monitoring the vehicle's position and applying corrective steering to keep it centered in the lane. These systems enhance driving safety by preventing accidents caused by unintentional lane departures.</p></p>
