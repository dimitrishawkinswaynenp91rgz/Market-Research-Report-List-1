<p><p>Metal forming lubricants are used to reduce friction and heat during the metal forming process. There are four main types of metal forming lubricants: water-based, oil-based, semisynthetic, and synthetic. </p><p>Water-based lubricants are environmentally friendly and provide good cooling properties. Oil-based lubricants offer excellent lubrication and film strength. Semisynthetic lubricants combine the benefits of both water-based and oil-based lubricants. Synthetic lubricants are highly advanced and offer superior lubrication and performance. Each type of lubricant has its own unique properties and applications in the metal forming industry.</p></p>
