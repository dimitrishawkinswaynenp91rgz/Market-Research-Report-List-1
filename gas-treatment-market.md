<p><strong>The Gas Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amines</li><li>Non-Amines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas treatment market can be categorized into two types: amines and non-amines. Amines are organic compounds that have a nitrogen atom and can react with acidic gases, such as carbon dioxide (CO2) and hydrogen sulfide (H2S), to form stable compounds. Amines are widely used in gas treatment processes to remove these acidic gases from natural gas and refinery gases. On the other hand, non-amines refer to alternative chemicals or technologies used to treat gases without the use of amines, offering different advantages and applications depending on the specific requirements of the gas treatment process.</p></p>
