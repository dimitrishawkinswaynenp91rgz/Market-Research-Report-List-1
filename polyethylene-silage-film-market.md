<p><ul><li>LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene)</li><li>LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyethylene silage film market is divided into three main types: LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), and Others. LLDPE silage films are known for their high puncture resistance and elongation properties, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. LDPE silage films offer good cling properties and are commonly used for wrapping round bales. The Others segment includes specialty polyethylene films with unique characteristics, such as enhanced UV resistance or biodegradability, catering to specific market needs.</p></p>
