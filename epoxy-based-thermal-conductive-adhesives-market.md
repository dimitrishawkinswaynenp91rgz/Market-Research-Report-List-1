<p><p>Epoxy Based Thermal Conductive Adhesives are categorized into three types based on their thermal conductivity: High Thermal Conductivity, Medium Thermal Conductivity, and Low Thermal Conductivity. High Thermal Conductivity Epoxy Based Adhesives have the highest ability to transfer heat, making them suitable for applications where thermal management is crucial. Medium Thermal Conductivity Adhesives offer moderate heat transfer properties, while Low Thermal Conductivity Adhesives have the lowest heat transfer capabilities, making them suitable for applications where heat dissipation is not a primary concern.</p></p>
