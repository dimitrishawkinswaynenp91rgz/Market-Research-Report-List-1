<p><p>Dual-arm robots are divided into two main market types: Transversely Articulated and Longitudinal Articulated. Transversely articulated robots have arms that move horizontally, allowing for greater flexibility in reaching different areas. Longitudinal articulated robots have arms that move vertically, providing better precision and control over specific tasks. Both types of robots have their own advantages depending on the application, with transversely articulated robots being more versatile in reaching different angles and longitudinal articulated robots being more precise in controlled movements.</p></p>
