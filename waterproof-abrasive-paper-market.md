<p><p>Waterproof abrasive paper comes in two main types: Waterproof Latex Paper and Waterproof Kraft Paper. Waterproof Latex Paper is coated with a latex adhesive that makes it flexible and resistant to water, ideal for wet sanding applications. On the other hand, Waterproof Kraft Paper is made from a strong kraft paper backing that offers durability and tear resistance, making it suitable for dry sanding tasks. Both types of waterproof abrasive paper are commonly used in various industries for sanding and finishing applications.</p></p>
