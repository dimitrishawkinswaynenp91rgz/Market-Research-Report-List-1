This machine allows physicians to guide instruments, such as catheters or needles, into the correct position for diagnosis or treatment while minimizing radiation exposure to the patient and healthcare staff.</p><p>The interventional fluoroscopy X-ray machine market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
