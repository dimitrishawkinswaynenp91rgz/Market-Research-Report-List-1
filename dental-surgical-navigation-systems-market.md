<p><p>Dental surgical navigation systems are advanced technological tools used in dental surgeries. They can be categorized into three types: optical, electromagnetic (EM), and hybrid systems. Optical systems use cameras to track the position of instruments in real-time. Electromagnetic systems use magnetic fields and sensors to track the instruments. Hybrid systems combine both optical and electromagnetic technologies for a more accurate and efficient tracking mechanism. These systems aid dental professionals in precise planning, guidance, and execution of dental procedures, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
