<p><strong>The Micro Microphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ECM (Electret Capacitance Microphone)</li><li>MEMS Microphone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ECM (Electret Capacitance Microphone) is a type of microphone that uses a permanently charged material to produce an electric signal when sound waves hit the diaphragm. This technology is widely used in consumer electronics like smartphones, laptops, and headsets. On the other hand, MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) microphones are smaller and more energy-efficient than traditional ECM microphones, making them ideal for applications in wearables, IoT devices, and medical equipment. They have gained popularity in recent years for their compact size and high performance.</p></p>
