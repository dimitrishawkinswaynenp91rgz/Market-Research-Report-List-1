<p><strong>The Dual-Reflective Window Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TSER ≥60%</li><li>TSER ≥70%</li><li>TSER ≥80%</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dual-Reflective Window Films are classified into three types based on Total Solar Energy Rejected (TSER) percentages: TSER ≥60%, TSER ≥70%, and TSER ≥80%. TSER ≥60% films block at least 60% of solar energy, providing moderate heat rejection. TSER ≥70% films offer higher heat rejection by blocking at least 70% of solar energy. TSER ≥80% films provide the highest level of heat rejection by blocking at least 80% of solar energy. Consumers can choose the appropriate film based on their specific heat rejection needs.</p></p>
