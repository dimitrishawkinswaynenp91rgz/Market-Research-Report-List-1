<p><p>Automated sample digestion systems are used in laboratories for breaking down samples into their basic components for analysis. There are two main types of systems in the market: Microwave Digestion Systems and Hotblock Digestion Systems. Microwave digestion systems use microwave radiation to heat samples rapidly and efficiently, while Hotblock digestion systems use heated blocks to digest samples. Both systems offer quick and reliable digestion of samples, making them popular choices in analytical laboratories.</p></p>
