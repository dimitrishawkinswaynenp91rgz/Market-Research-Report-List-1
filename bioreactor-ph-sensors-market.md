<p><p>Bioreactor pH sensors can be classified into three main types: glass type sensors, ISFET sensors, and others. Glass type sensors are traditional and commonly used in bioreactors for measuring pH levels. ISFET sensors are advanced technology sensors that offer high accuracy and durability. Other types of pH sensors may include optical sensors or polymer membrane sensors. Each type of sensor has its own advantages and applications in bioreactor systems, allowing for precise monitoring and control of pH levels for optimal cultivation of cells or microorganisms.</p></p>
