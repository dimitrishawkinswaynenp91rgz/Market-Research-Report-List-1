<p><p>The skin grafting system market consists of different types of devices used for harvesting healthy skin from one area of the body and transplanting it onto another area. Knife Dermatomes use a surgical blade to remove the skin graft, while Drum Dermatomes consist of a rotating drum that does the same. Electrical Dermatomes use an electrical motor to create precise skin grafts, and Air-powered Dermatomes use compressed air to cut the graft. These devices enable surgeons to perform skin grafting procedures efficiently and accurately.</p></p>
