Granular ETFE resin is typically used in injection molding applications, providing high strength and excellent chemical resistance. Powder ETFE resin is commonly used in powder coating processes, offering good adhesion and abrasion resistance properties. Both types of ETFE resin are versatile materials with a wide range of applications in industries such as construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
