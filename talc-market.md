<p><p>Talc market can be classified into two main types: Talc Lump and Talc Powder. Talc Lump refers to the natural form of talc extracted from mines and has various industrial applications. Talc Powder, on the other hand, is processed talc that is finely ground to create a smooth powder texture often used in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and other consumer products. Both types of talc products serve different purposes and cater to distinct industries within the market.</p></p>
