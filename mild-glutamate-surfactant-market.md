<p><p>Mild glutamate surfactants are a type of cleansing agent used in personal care products. Two common types are Cocoyl Glutamate and Lauroyl Glutamate. Cocoyl Glutamate is derived from coconut oil and is known for its gentle, non-irritating properties. Lauroyl Glutamate is derived from glutamic acid and lauric acid, offering similar mildness and effectiveness. Both types are popular in the market for their ability to cleanse the skin without stripping it of natural oils, making them suitable for sensitive skin types.</p></p>
