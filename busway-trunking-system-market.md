<p><p>Busway trunking systems are classified into two main types based on the material used for conducting electricity - Aluminium Busway Trunking System and Copper Busway Trunking System. Aluminium busway trunking systems are lightweight and cost-effective, making them suitable for low to medium power distribution. On the other hand, Copper busway trunking systems are known for their high conductivity and heat resistance, making them ideal for high power applications where reliability and efficiency are critical. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the installation.</p></p>
