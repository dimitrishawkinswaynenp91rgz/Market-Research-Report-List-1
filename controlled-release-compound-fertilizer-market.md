<p><p>Controlled-release compound fertilizers are designed to slowly release nutrients to plants over an extended period of time, improving efficiency and reducing the risk of nutrient leaching. The market is divided into two main types: polymer coated compound fertilizers and non-polymer coated compound fertilizers. Polymer coated fertilizers are coated with a thin layer of polymer that controls the release of nutrients, while non-polymer coated fertilizers rely on chemical reactions or physical barriers to regulate nutrient release. Both types offer benefits in improving crop productivity and minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
