<p><p>Modified bitumen waterproof membrane market can be divided into two main types: SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) and APP (Atactic Polypropylene). SBS membranes are known for their flexibility, durability, and resistance to high temperatures, making them ideal for areas with extreme weather conditions. On the other hand, APP membranes offer excellent UV resistance and aging properties, making them suitable for applications where exposure to sunlight is a concern. Both types of membranes provide reliable waterproofing solutions for various construction projects.</p></p>
