<p><p>A headless ecommerce system is a type of ecommerce platform that separates the front-end (user interface) from the back-end (server and database) of an online store. This architecture allows for greater flexibility and customization, as the front-end can be designed and developed independently from the back-end, enabling businesses to deliver unique and personalized user experiences.</p><p>In a headless ecommerce system, information is passed through APIs (Application Programming Interfaces) between the front-end and back-end, allowing for seamless integration with various channels and devices such as mobile apps, voice assistants, smart TVs, social media platforms, and more. This enables businesses to deliver consistent and engaging shopping experiences across different touchpoints.</p><p>The headless ecommerce system market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for personalized and omnichannel shopping experiences.
