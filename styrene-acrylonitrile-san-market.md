<p><p>Styrene Acrylonitrile (SAN) is a copolymer resin widely used in various applications. Injection Grade SAN is specifically designed for injection molding processes, providing excellent flow properties and dimensional stability. On the other hand, General Grade SAN is a versatile material suitable for extrusion, thermoforming, and other processes. Both types of SAN offer high impact strength, chemical resistance, and good heat resistance. Companies can choose the appropriate grade based on their specific manufacturing requirements and end-use applications.</p></p>
