<p><p>Collaboration and productivity software can be categorized into two market types - on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise software is installed and operated on the organization's own servers and infrastructure. It requires maintenance and updates to be done by in-house IT teams. On the other hand, cloud-based software is accessed over the internet, hosted on third-party servers. It offers flexibility, scalability, and automatic updates, without the need for on-site infrastructure. Cloud-based software allows collaboration and increased productivity by enabling remote access and real-time collaboration across teams and locations.</p></p>
