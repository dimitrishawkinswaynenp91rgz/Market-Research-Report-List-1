<p><p>The nasal flu vaccine market consists of two types: trivalent flu vaccine and quadrivalent flu vaccine. The trivalent flu vaccine protects against three strains of the flu virus, typically two influenza A strains and one influenza B strain. On the other hand, the quadrivalent flu vaccine protects against four strains, including two influenza A strains and two influenza B strains. Both vaccines are administered through the nose and are used to prevent influenza infections. They are widely available and recommended during flu seasons.</p></p>
