The types of flame retardants commonly used in the market include: ATH (aluminum trihydrate), Antimony Oxide, Brominated, Chlorinated, and Phosphorous. ATH is a non-toxic and environmentally friendly option, while Antimony Oxide is effective in small amounts. Brominated and Chlorinated flame retardants are known for their high efficiency, but there are concerns about their impact on health and the environment. Phosphorous flame retardants are gaining popularity due to their lower toxicity levels.</p></p>
