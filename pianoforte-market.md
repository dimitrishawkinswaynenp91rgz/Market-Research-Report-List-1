<p><p>The pianoforte market is divided into two main types: grand pianos and upright pianos. Grand pianos are large, horizontal pianos with a soundboard and strings that run horizontally, providing a rich and resonant sound. They are often used in concert halls and by professional pianists. Upright pianos, on the other hand, are more compact and vertically oriented, making them suitable for smaller spaces such as homes and music studios.
