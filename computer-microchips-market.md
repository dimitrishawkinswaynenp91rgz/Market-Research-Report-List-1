<p><p>Computer microchips market types can be categorized into analog chips and digital chips. Analog chips are designed to process continuous electrical signals and are commonly used in audio and video equipment, power management systems, and sensors. Digital chips, on the other hand, process discrete electrical signals and are widely used in computers, smartphones, and other digital devices. While analog chips focus on smooth signal processing and accurate voltage measurements, digital chips excel at rapid computations and logic operations required in binary systems.</p></p>
