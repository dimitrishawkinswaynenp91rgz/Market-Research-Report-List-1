<p><p>E. Coli testing market encompasses different types of technology including monoclonal antibodies, molecular diagnostics, immunoassays, and differential light scattering. Monoclonal antibodies are used to detect specific antigens of E. Coli bacteria. Molecular diagnostics focus on the genetic material of the bacteria for identification. Immunoassays rely on the reaction between antigens and antibodies for detection. Differential light scattering measures changes in light when it interacts with bacterial cells. Each of these technologies plays a vital role in the accurate and efficient testing of E. Coli.</p></p>
