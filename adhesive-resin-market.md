<p><p>Adhesive resin market types include water-based, solvent-based, hot melt, reactive, and others. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. Solvent-based adhesives offer high bonding strength but may have harmful volatile organic compounds. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature and melt when heated, providing fast and strong bonds. Reactive adhesives form bonds through a chemical reaction with a curing agent. Other types of adhesives may include pressure-sensitive, structural, and UV-curable adhesives. Each type offers unique characteristics for various applications.</p></p>
