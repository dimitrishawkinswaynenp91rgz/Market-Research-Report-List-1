<p><p>The automotive nuts market consists of various types such as hub nuts, flange nuts, lock nuts, and other nuts. Hub nuts are used to secure wheels to the axle and are commonly found in wheel hubs. Flange nuts have a wide flange that acts as a washer to distribute pressure and prevent loosening. Lock nuts have a special design to prevent vibration-induced loosening. Other nuts in the market include hex nuts, Nyloc nuts, and wing nuts, each with their unique applications in automotive assembly.</p></p>
