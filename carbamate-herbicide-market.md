Molinate is commonly used to control grassy weeds, while thiobencarb is effective against broadleaf weeds. Phenmedipham is known for its pre-emergent control of annual grasses, and prosulfocarb is used to control grasses and broadleaf weeds. Each type of carbamate herbicide offers different properties and benefits for weed control in various crops.</p></p>
