<p><p>Location-Based Services (LBS) and Real-Time Location Systems (RTLS) are technologies that use a combination of hardware and software to track the location of people, objects, or assets in real-time. LBS leverage GPS and other location technologies to offer services based on the current location of a user, such as navigation, local search, and social networking. RTLS, on the other hand, use technologies like RFID, Bluetooth, and Wi-Fi to track the real-time location of assets within a defined area, providing organizations with valuable insights into their operations.</p><p>The Location-Based Services and Real-Time Location Systems Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing adoption of location-based technologies across various industries.
