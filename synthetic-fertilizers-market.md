<p><p>Synthetic fertilizers are divided into four main types based on their nutrient content - nitrogen, phosphorus, potash, and micronutrients. Nitrogen fertilizers are used to promote leafy green growth, phosphorus is essential for root development and flowering, potash enhances plant resistance and fruit formation, while micronutrients like iron, manganese, and zinc support overall plant health and development. Each type of synthetic fertilizer plays a crucial role in providing plants with the necessary nutrients for optimal growth and productivity.</p></p>
