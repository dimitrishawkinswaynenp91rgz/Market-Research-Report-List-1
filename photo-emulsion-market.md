<p><p>Photo emulsion markets can be classified into different types based on their composition and characteristics. Dichromate emulsions use potassium dichromate as a sensitizer, while SBQ-based emulsions are made using diazonium salts. Diazo emulsions utilize diazo compounds to create a light-sensitive layer. Other types of emulsions may include hybrid or specialty formulations that offer unique properties or applications. Each type of emulsion offers specific advantages and is chosen based on the desired outcome of the printing process.</p></p>
