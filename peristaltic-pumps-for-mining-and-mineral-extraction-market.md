Two main types of peristaltic pumps used in this market are roller peristaltic pumps and shoe type peristaltic pumps. Roller peristaltic pumps use rotating rollers to compress a flexible tube, while shoe type peristaltic pumps use a shoe or shoe-like element to compress the tube. Both types are effective in pumping slurries, chemicals, and other materials commonly found in mining and mineral extraction processes.</p></p>
