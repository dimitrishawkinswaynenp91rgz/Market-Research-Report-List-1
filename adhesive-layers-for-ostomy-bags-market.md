<p><p>Adhesive layers for ostomy bags are essential components that help secure the bag in place, preventing leakages and ensuring comfort for the user. The different types of adhesive layers include stoma paste, flanges, flange extenders, stoma rings, barrier film, adhesive remover, and others. Stoma paste is used to fill in uneven skin surfaces for a secure fit, while flanges provide additional support. Flange extenders help extend the wear time of the bag, while barrier film protects the skin from irritation. Adhesive remover aids in painless removal of the bag.</p></p>
