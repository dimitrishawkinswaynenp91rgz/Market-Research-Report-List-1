<p><p>Auto Transformer Rectifier Unit (ATRU) is a device used for converting AC power to DC power in industries such as mining and railways. It comes in three main types: 6 Pulse, 12 Pulse, and 18 Pulse. These types differ in the number of pulses they use to rectify AC power. 6 Pulse ATRUs are the most common and cost-effective, while 12 Pulse and 18 Pulse ATRUs offer improved efficiency and reduced harmonic distortion. Each type caters to different needs and requirements of industries.</p></p>
