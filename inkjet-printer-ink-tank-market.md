<p><p>In the split ink tank market, the printer comes with individual ink tanks for different colors. This allows users to replace only the specific color that runs out, leading to cost savings. On the other hand, in the all-in-one ink tank market, the printer comes with a single ink tank that holds all colors. While this may be more convenient, replacing the entire tank when one color runs out can be more expensive in the long run.</p></p>
