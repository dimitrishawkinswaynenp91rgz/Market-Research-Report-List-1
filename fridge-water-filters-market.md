<p><p>Fridge water filters come in three main types: Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) Filters, Carbon Block Filters, and Other. GAC filters use a bed of activated carbon granules to remove impurities, while carbon block filters have a solid block of carbon that traps contaminants. Other types of fridge water filters may include reverse osmosis filters or ceramic filters. Each type of filter offers unique benefits and is designed to improve the quality and taste of the water dispensed from the fridge.</p></p>
