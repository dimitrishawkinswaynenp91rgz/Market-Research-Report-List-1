<p><p>Masonry adhesives are widely categorized into different types based on their chemical composition and properties. Acrylic adhesive offers excellent bonding strength and weather resistance. Polyurethane adhesive provides strong adhesion to various substrates and is durable in different weather conditions. Polyvinyl acetate adhesive is known for its fast bonding properties and ease of use. Epoxy adhesive offers high strength and is suitable for bonding heavy materials. Other types of masonry adhesives include hybrid and solvent-based adhesives, each catering to specific applications and requirements.</p></p>
