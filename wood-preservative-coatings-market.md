<p><p>Wood preservative coatings can be categorized into three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and others. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly and easy to apply, making them popular among consumers. Solvent-based coatings provide durable protection against moisture and are commonly used for outdoor applications. The "others" category may include oil-based coatings or hybrid formulations that offer unique benefits such as enhanced UV protection or improved adhesion to different types of wood. Each type of coating has its own advantages and suitability for specific applications.</p></p>
