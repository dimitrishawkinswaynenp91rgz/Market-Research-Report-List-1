Advanced features such as improved depth perception, high-resolution imaging, and integrated video recording systems have increased the demand for these microscopes in ophthalmic surgeries.</p><p>The market is also fueled by the growing adoption of minimally invasive surgical techniques and increasing investments in healthcare infrastructure. Minimally invasive surgeries offer advantages such as reduced risk of infection, shorter hospital stays, and faster recovery times. These technologies enable real-time assessment of tissue health and aid in precise surgical decision-making.</p><p>Overall, the advanced ophthalmic operating microscopes market is projected to experience significant growth, driven by the rising prevalence of eye diseases, technological advancements, and the increasing adoption of minimally invasive ophthalmic surgeries.
