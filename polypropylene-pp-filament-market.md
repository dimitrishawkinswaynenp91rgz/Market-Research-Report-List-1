<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) Filament market types include Polypropylene Multifilament and Polypropylene Monofilament. Polypropylene Multifilament refers to a type of PP filament made up of multiple thin fibers twisted together, providing strength and durability. On the other hand, Polypropylene Monofilament is a single, solid strand of PP filament, known for its smooth texture and ability to hold colors well. Both market types cater to different needs and applications within the textile and industrial sectors.</p></p>
