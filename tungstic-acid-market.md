<p><p>Tungstic acid is commonly available in two forms in the market - Monohydrate and Hemihydrate. Monohydrate tungstic acid is the form with one molecule of water per molecule of tungstic acid, while Hemihydrate contains half a molecule of water per molecule of tungstic acid. These two forms have different properties and applications in various industries. Monohydrate is often used in the production of catalysts, pigments, and ceramics, while Hemihydrate is utilized in the manufacturing of tungsten compounds and chemicals.</p></p>
