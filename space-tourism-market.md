<p><p>Space tourism can be classified into two main market types: suborbital and orbital. Suborbital tourism involves relatively short trips, where passengers reach space but do not complete a full orbit around the Earth. This type of tourism offers a few minutes of weightlessness and spectacular views before returning to Earth. On the other hand, orbital tourism involves longer journeys, where passengers travel into Earth's orbit and remain there for an extended period. This type of tourism allows for a more immersive experience and the opportunity to live and work in space for a certain duration.</p></p>
