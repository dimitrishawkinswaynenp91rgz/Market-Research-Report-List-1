<p><p>Bioplastic composites are classified into two main types - natural polymer and synthetic polymer markets. The natural polymer market consists of biodegradable materials derived from plant-based sources such as corn starch, sugarcane, and cellulose. These bioplastics are eco-friendly alternatives to traditional plastics and are gaining popularity due to their sustainability. On the other hand, the synthetic polymer market includes bioplastics made from petrochemicals or other non-renewable sources, which offer enhanced durability and performance but are not biodegradable.</p></p>
