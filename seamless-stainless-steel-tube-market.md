<p><p>The Seamless Stainless Steel Tube market is categorized into two main types: Cold Finished Tube and Hot Finished Tube. Cold Finished Tube undergoes final finishing processes at room temperature, resulting in smooth and precise dimensions. On the other hand, Hot Finished Tube is produced at high temperatures, resulting in a rougher surface finish but with improved mechanical properties. Both types of tubes are widely used in various industries such as automotive, oil and gas, and construction for their durability, corrosion resistance, and high strength.</p></p>
