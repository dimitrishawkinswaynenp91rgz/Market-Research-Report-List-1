<p><p>Biomass pyrolysis oil market primarily consists of two types: fast pyrolysis and flash pyrolysis. Fast pyrolysis involves quickly heating biomass material to high temperatures in the absence of oxygen, resulting in a higher yield of liquid oil. Flash pyrolysis, on the other hand, involves rapidly heating biomass at extremely high temperatures for a shorter duration, leading to a more efficient conversion process. Both market types play a significant role in producing bio-oil as a renewable energy source.</p></p>
