<p><p>The scaffold material market can be categorized into different types based on their design and usage. Supported scaffolding is commonly used for construction projects and is held upright by poles, frames, or braces. Suspended scaffolding is suspended from the top of a building or structure and is typically used for tasks like window washing or painting. Rolling scaffolding is movable and can be easily repositioned, making it ideal for projects that require frequent movement. Other types of scaffolding include mobile scaffolding, cantilever scaffolding, and aerial lifts.</p></p>
