<p><p>The spiral chute market can be categorized into two types: powered spiral chute and unpowered spiral chute. A powered spiral chute utilizes an electrical motor to rotate the spiral pathway, aiding the movement of materials from one level to another. In contrast, an unpowered spiral chute relies on gravity and the design of its spiral pathway to facilitate material transfer. Both types serve as efficient and safe solutions for conveying materials vertically, such as in warehouses, manufacturing plants, and logistics centers.</p></p>
