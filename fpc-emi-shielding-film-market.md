<p><ul><li>Conductive Adhesive Type</li><li>Metal Alloy Type</li><li>Metal Microneedle Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The FPC EMI Shielding Film market includes three main types: Conductive Adhesive Type, Metal Alloy Type, and Metal Microneedle Type. Conductive Adhesive Type shields against EMI by utilizing a conductive adhesive layer. Metal Alloy Type uses a blend of metals to provide effective shielding against electromagnetic interference. Metal Microneedle Type utilizes tiny metal needles to create a barrier against EMI. These different types cater to various application requirements and provide reliable EMI shielding for flexible printed circuits.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Single-sided Circuit</li><li>Double-sided Circuit</li><li>Multi-layer Circuit</li><li>Rigid-Flex Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FPC EMI Shielding Film is used in various circuit applications including single-sided, double-sided, multi-layer, and rigid-flex circuits. In single-sided circuits, the film is applied to one side of the circuit board to block electromagnetic interference. Double-sided circuits have shielding film on both sides for increased protection. Multi-layer circuits utilize the film between layers to prevent interference across the board. Rigid-flex circuits combine flexible circuits with rigid sections, requiring shielding film for overall EMI protection.</p></p>
