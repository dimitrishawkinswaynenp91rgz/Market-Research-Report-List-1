<p><p>Bentonite clay market can be categorized into three main types: Sodium Bentonite, Calcium Bentonite, and Potassium Bentonite. Sodium Bentonite is known for its high swelling capability and is commonly used in drilling muds and construction applications. Calcium Bentonite, on the other hand, is valued for its absorbent properties and is popular in the beauty and wellness industry. Potassium Bentonite is less common but has similar uses to Sodium Bentonite. Each type has unique qualities that cater to different industries and applications.</p></p>
