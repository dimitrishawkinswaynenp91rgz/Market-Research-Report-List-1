<p><p>Air spring assemblies are used in various applications to provide suspension and support. There are three main types of air spring assemblies: corrugated bellows, rolling cam bellows, and sleeve bellows. Corrugated bellows utilize a series of convolutions to provide flexibility and support. Rolling cam bellows use a rolling lobe design to control spring rate and suspension stiffness. Sleeve bellows consist of a flexible sleeve that expands and contracts to provide cushioning and support. Each type offers unique features and benefits for different applications.</p></p>
