<p><p>Float switch sensors can be classified into three main types based on their mounting position and design: Top-Mounted Type, Side-Mounted Type, and Special Type. Top-Mounted Type sensors are installed at the top of a tank or container, while Side-Mounted Type sensors are mounted on the side.
