Digital truck scales use advanced technology for precise weight measurement and data storage capabilities. Mechanical truck scales use mechanical components like levers or springs to measure weight. Other types of truck scales may include hydraulic truck scales or portable truck scales, each offering unique features and benefits for different applications.</p></p>
