<p><p>A laminated glass autoclave is a type of pressure vessel used in the manufacturing process of laminated safety glass. Laminated glass consists of two or more layers of glass with an interlayer of polyvinyl butyral (PVB) or ethylene-vinyl acetate (EVA) resin. The autoclave applies heat and pressure to bond the layers together, creating a strong and durable glass product with enhanced safety features.</p><p>The laminated glass autoclave market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period. The growth of the market can be attributed to the increasing demand for safety glass in the automotive, construction, and aerospace industries.
