<p><ul><li>On-Grid String Inverter</li><li>Off-Grid String Inverter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The string inverter market can be categorized into two types: on-grid and off-grid string inverters. On-grid string inverters are primarily used in solar power systems that are connected to the electrical grid. They convert the direct current (DC) produced by solar panels into alternating current (AC) to be supplied to the grid. Off-grid string inverters, on the other hand, are used in standalone solar power systems that are not connected to the grid. They convert DC power from solar panels into AC power for immediate use or storage in batteries.</p></p>
