<p><p>Flange gasket sheets are available in three main types based on their material composition: metallic, semi-metallic, and non-metallic. Metallic gaskets are made of materials like stainless steel and are known for their high strength and temperature resistance. Semi-metallic gaskets combine metal with non-metallic materials for a balance of flexibility and durability. Non-metallic gaskets are made of materials like rubber or graphite, offering good sealing properties while being cost-effective. Each type of gasket sheet has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
