<p><strong>The Automotive Acceleration Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoresistive</li><li>Capacitive</li><li>Servo-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive acceleration sensor market is composed of various types, including the piezoresistive, capacitive, and servo-type sensors. Piezoresistive sensors utilize the change in resistance when subjected to mechanical stress, accurately measuring acceleration. Capacitive sensors measure acceleration by detecting changes in capacitance between two plates. Servo-type sensors operate based on a current proportional to acceleration, offering precise measurement. Each sensor type caters to different automotive applications, providing reliable data necessary for vehicle control and safety systems.</p></p>
