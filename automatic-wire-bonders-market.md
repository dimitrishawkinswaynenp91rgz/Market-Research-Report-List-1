There are two main types of automatic wire bonders: Semi-automatic wire bonders and Fully-automatic wire bonders. Semi-automatic wire bonders require some manual intervention for loading/unloading parts, while fully-automatic wire bonders can operate without any human assistance. Both types of wire bonders are essential in the production of electronic devices and help improve efficiency and precision in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
