<p><p>Vacuum tire market consists of two main types: radial tires and slash tires. Radial tires have a layer of cords at a 90-degree angle to the direction of travel, providing better stability and durability. They are popular for their efficient performance, longer lifespan, and comfortable ride. On the other hand, slash tires have a pattern of diagonal cords, which offer superior traction and grip on uneven surfaces. They are commonly used for off-road and rugged terrain applications due to their enhanced capability to handle rough conditions.</p></p>
