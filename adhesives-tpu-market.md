<p><ul><li>Water Soluble TPU Adhesive</li><li>Water Emulsion TPU Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-based TPU Adhesive</li><li>Solid TPU Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Adhesives TPU market types include water soluble TPU adhesive, water emulsion TPU adhesive, solvent-based TPU adhesive, and solid TPU adhesive. Water soluble TPU adhesive is easily dissolved in water, making it environmentally friendly. Water emulsion TPU adhesive is a stable dispersion of TPU particles in water, providing good bonding properties. Solvent-based TPU adhesive offers high strength and fast curing time. Solid TPU adhesive is in solid form and requires heat activation for bonding. Each type caters to different applications and preferences in the adhesive industry.</p></p>
