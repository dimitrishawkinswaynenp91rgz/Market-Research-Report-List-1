<p><p>Silicone elastomers are commonly used in various medical applications due to their unique properties. There are three types of silicone elastomer markets: HTV (High Temperature Vulcanizing), RTV (Room Temperature Vulcanizing), and LSR (Liquid Silicone Rubber). HTV elastomers require high heat for curing, while RTV elastomers cure at room temperature through a chemical reaction. LSR elastomers are liquids that cure into flexible solids. These different types of silicone elastomers offer varying curing processes, allowing manufacturers to choose the most suitable option based on the specific requirements of medical applications.</p></p>
