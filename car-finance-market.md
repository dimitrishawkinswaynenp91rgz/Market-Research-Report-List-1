<p><p>Car finance market types refer to the various durations or lengths of time in which a borrower can repay their car loan. The most common market types include 24 months, 36 months, 48 months, 60 months, and 72 months. These numbers represent the number of months it will take to fully repay the loan. Shorter-term options like 24 or 36 months typically result in higher monthly payments but less overall interest paid, while longer-term options like 60 or 72 months may have lower monthly payments but result in more interest paid over time. Some lenders may also offer other market types not mentioned above, which can vary in duration depending on the lender's terms and the borrower's financial situation.</p></p>
