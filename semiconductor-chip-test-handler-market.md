<p><p>Semiconductor Chip Test Handlers are used in the testing of semiconductor chips. There are four main types of test handlers in the market. Gravity Handlers rely on gravity to move and position chips for testing. Turret Handlers use a rotating turret to position chips. Pick-and-Place Handlers use robotic arms to pick up and place chips. Strip Handlers are designed to handle multiple chips at once in a strip format. Each handler type offers unique benefits and capabilities for semiconductor testing processes.</p></p>
