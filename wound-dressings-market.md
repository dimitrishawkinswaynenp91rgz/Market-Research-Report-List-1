<p><p>The wound dressings market offers a variety of types to cater to different wound healing needs. Films provide a transparent barrier for protection. Foam dressings absorb drainage and provide cushioning. Collagens promote tissue growth and healing. Alginates are derived from seaweed and absorb exudates. Hydrocolloids create a moist environment for healing. Hydrogels provide cooling relief and maintain moisture. Super absorbers are highly absorbent dressings for heavily draining wounds. Each type serves a specific purpose in promoting wound healing.</p></p>
