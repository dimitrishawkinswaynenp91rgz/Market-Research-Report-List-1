<p><p>Conductive silver powder is used in various industries for its high electrical conductivity. Two common types of conductive silver powder are flake silver powder and spherical silver powder. Flake silver powder is characterized by its irregular, flat shape, which allows for greater particle-to-particle contact and conductivity. Spherical silver powder, on the other hand, is made up of rounded particles that offer better flow properties and pack tightly together. Both types have specific applications based on their unique properties in the market.</p></p>
