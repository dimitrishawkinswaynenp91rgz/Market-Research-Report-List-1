The market is segmented into different types including shell & tube heat exchangers, plate heat exchangers, air cooled heat exchangers, and others. Shell & tube heat exchangers consist of a shell containing multiple tubes for heat transfer. Plate heat exchangers have metal plates with fluid channels for efficient heat exchange. Air cooled heat exchangers use air to cool the fluids. Other types include spiral, finned tube, and double pipe heat exchangers.</p></p>
