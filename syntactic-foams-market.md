<p><p>Syntactic foams are available in three main forms: sheet form, rod form, and block form. Sheet form syntactic foams are typically used in applications requiring a large surface area, such as thermal insulation. Rod form syntactic foams are suitable for applications where high compressive strength is needed, such as underwater buoyancy systems. Block form syntactic foams are commonly used in aerospace and marine industries for their lightweight and high-strength properties. Each form offers specific benefits for various applications in different industries.</p></p>
