Pigments are insoluble colorants added to materials to give them color. Dyes are soluble colorants used to color materials through absorption. Masterbatches are concentrated mixtures of pigments used to color plastics during processing. Color concentrates are similar to masterbatches but used for a wider range of materials. Additionally, there are other specialized colorants used in various industries for different applications.</p></p>
