<p><p>Product Data Management (PDM) software is available in two types: on-premise and cloud-based. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as the software provider manages updates and infrastructure. In the cloud-based market, businesses can access and manage their product data anytime and anywhere, making collaboration and data sharing easier.</p></p>
