<p><ul><li>Flat Floor Mats</li><li>Molded Floor Mats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle floor mats come in two main types: flat floor mats and molded floor mats. </p><p>Flat floor mats are usually made of rubber, carpet, or vinyl and are flexible and easy to trim to fit various vehicle sizes. They provide basic protection against dirt and spills, but may not offer as precise coverage as molded floor mats.</p><p>Molded floor mats are custom-designed to fit specific vehicle models and are often made of heavy-duty rubber or polymer materials. They provide full coverage and protection for the vehicle floor, with raised edges to contain spills and debris.</p></p>
