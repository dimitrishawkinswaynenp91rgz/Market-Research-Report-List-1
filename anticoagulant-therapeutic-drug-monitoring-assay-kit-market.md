<p><p>The market for Anticoagulant Therapeutic Drug Monitoring Assay Kits can be categorized into three types: Fluorescence Polarization Immunoassay, Homogeneous Enzyme Immunoassay, and Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Assay. Fluorescence Polarization Immunoassay measures the changes in fluorescence properties to detect drug levels. Homogeneous Enzyme Immunoassay involves simple reaction steps and is suitable for high-throughput screening. Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Assay utilizes antibodies and enzymes to detect and measure drug concentrations. These different assay kits offer varying methods to accurately monitor the levels of anticoagulant drugs in patients.</p></p>
