<p><p>The Electronic Suspension System Market includes two main types: Semi-Active Suspension System and Active Suspension System. Semi-Active Suspension Systems use electronically controlled dampers to adjust the suspension based on driving conditions, offering a balance between comfort and performance. Active Suspension Systems, on the other hand, use sensors to constantly monitor and adjust the suspension in real time, providing the best possible ride quality and handling. Both systems offer improved stability, comfort, and performance compared to traditional passive suspension systems.</p></p>
