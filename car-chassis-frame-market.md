<p><ul><li>Load Bearing</li><li>Non-loaded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car chassis frame market can be divided into two main types: load bearing and non-loaded. Load bearing chassis frames support the weight of the vehicle and its occupants, transmitting forces from the suspension and engine to the wheels. Non-loaded chassis frames, on the other hand, do not bear the weight of the vehicle and are typically used in lightweight or specialty vehicles. Both types play a crucial role in providing structural integrity and stability to the vehicle.</p></p>
