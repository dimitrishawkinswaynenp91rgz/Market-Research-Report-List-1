<p><p>Rock pickers are agricultural equipment used to remove rocks and debris from fields to prevent damage to farm machinery. There are three main market types for rock pickers: trailed, mounted, and semi-mounted. Trailed rock pickers are towed behind a tractor and are ideal for smaller fields. Mounted rock pickers are attached directly to a tractor and are better suited for larger fields. Semi-mounted rock pickers are a combination of the two, offering flexibility and efficiency. Each market type caters to different farming needs and field sizes.</p></p>
