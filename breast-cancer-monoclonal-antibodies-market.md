<p><p>The Breast Cancer Monoclonal Antibodies market includes two types of antibodies: Naked MAbs and Conjugated MAbs. Naked MAbs are monoclonal antibodies that are not linked to any other substance. They work by recognizing and binding to specific targets on breast cancer cells, aiding in immune response against the cancer. On the other hand, Conjugated MAbs are monoclonal antibodies that are linked to a chemotherapy drug or radioactive substance. This linkage allows the antibodies to specifically target and deliver the treatment directly to the cancer cells, increasing its effectiveness.</p></p>
