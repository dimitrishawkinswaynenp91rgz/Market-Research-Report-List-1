<p><p>Styrenic Block Copolymers (SBC) are a type of thermoplastic elastomer used in various industries due to their flexibility, durability, and versatility. There are three main types of SBC: Styrene-Butadiene Styrene (SBS), Styrene-Isoprene-Styrene (SIS), and Hydrogenated Styrenic Block Copolymer (HSBC). SBS is known for its high strength and abrasion resistance, SIS for its excellent clarity and tackiness, and HSBC for its improved thermal and oxidative stability. Each type has unique properties that make them suitable for different applications in the market.</p></p>
