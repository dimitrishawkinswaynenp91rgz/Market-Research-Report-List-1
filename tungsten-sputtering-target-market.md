<p><p>The Tungsten Sputtering Target market includes two main types: Rotary Type and Non-Rotatable Type. </p><p>Rotary Type targets are cylinder-shaped and can be rotated during the sputtering process, giving them longer lifetimes due to more even wear. </p><p>Non-Rotatable Type targets are typically rectangular or planar-shaped and do not rotate during sputtering but are easier to install and maintain. </p><p>Both types are widely used in the semiconductor, electronics, and optics industries for thin film deposition applications.</p></p>
