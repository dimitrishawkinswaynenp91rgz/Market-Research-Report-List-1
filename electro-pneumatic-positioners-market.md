<p><strong>The Electro-Pneumatic Positioners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electro-Pneumatic Single Acting Positioners</li><li>Electro-Pneumatic Double Acting Positioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electro-Pneumatic Single Acting Positioners utilize a single acting pneumatic actuator to control the position of a valve or damper in response to an electrical input signal. This type of positioner is commonly used in applications where a fail-safe position is required in the event of power loss. On the other hand, Electro-Pneumatic Double Acting Positioners use a double acting pneumatic actuator to provide precise control over the positioning of a valve or damper, making them suitable for applications requiring faster response times and greater accuracy.</p></p>
