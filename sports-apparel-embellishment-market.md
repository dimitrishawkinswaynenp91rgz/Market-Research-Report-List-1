<p><p>Sports apparel embellishment market involves various techniques such as screen printing, DTG printing, heat transfer printing, and embroidery. Screen printing involves pressing ink through a mesh screen onto the fabric. DTG printing uses specialized printers to directly apply ink onto garments. Heat transfer printing uses heat to transfer designs onto fabric. Embroidery involves stitching designs onto apparel. Each of these techniques offers different advantages in terms of cost, durability, and flexibility in customization for sports apparel.</p></p>
