<p><strong>The Malaria Vaccines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-Erythrocytic Vaccine</li><li>Erythrocytic Vaccine</li><li>Multi-antigen Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Malaria Vaccines Market Types can be classified into three categories. Firstly, Pre-Erythrocytic Vaccines aim to prevent the parasite from infecting the liver by targeting the early stages of the malaria lifecycle. Secondly, Erythrocytic Vaccines focus on preventing the parasite from multiplying and spreading throughout the blood. Lastly, Multi-antigen Vaccines use a combination of antigens to provide broad protection against various strains of the malaria parasite. These various types of vaccines contribute to the overall efforts of preventing and controlling malaria infections.</p></p>
