<p><p>Elastomeric impression materials are widely used in dentistry for making accurate dental impressions. The main types of elastomeric impression materials include addition silicone, condensation silicone, polyether, polysulfide, and others. Addition silicone is known for its accuracy and stability, while condensation silicone is economical and easy to use. Polyether offers high tear strength and detailed reproduction, while polysulfide is used for its good wetting properties. Other elastomeric impression materials include hybrid materials like vinyl polysiloxane. Each type has its own unique characteristics and advantages in the dental market.</p></p>
