<p><p>Plastic aspheric lenses market consists of various types such as compression molding, injection molding, and turning molding. Compression molding involves shaping the lens by compressing the material in a mold. Injection molding uses high pressure to inject molten plastic into a mold to form the lens. Turning molding involves cutting the lens material into the desired shape. Each method offers unique advantages in terms of cost, efficiency, and precision, catering to different needs in the plastic aspheric lenses market.</p></p>
