<p><strong>The PVC Artificial Leather Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Calender PVC Leather</li><li>Rotary Screen Coating PVC Artificial Leather</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Calender PVC leather is produced by passing the PVC material through a series of heated rollers to create a thin, flexible sheet of artificial leather. On the other hand, Rotary Screen Coating PVC Artificial Leather is created by applying a PVC coating onto a base fabric using a rotary screen printing technique. Both types of PVC artificial leather offer different textures, finishes, and characteristics, catering to various applications such as upholstery, clothing, accessories, and automotive interiors in the market.</p></p>
