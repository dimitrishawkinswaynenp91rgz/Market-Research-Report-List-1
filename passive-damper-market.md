<p><p>Passive dampers are classified into two main types - hydraulic type and pneumatic type. Hydraulic type dampers use hydraulic fluid to absorb and dissipate energy, while pneumatic type dampers use air or gas. Both types of dampers are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction. Hydraulic dampers are known for their high energy absorption capabilities, while pneumatic dampers are preferred for their lightweight and corrosion-resistant properties. Both types play a crucial role in damping vibrations and controlling motion in mechanical systems.</p></p>
