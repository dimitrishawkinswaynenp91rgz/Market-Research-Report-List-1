<p><p>In the dry bulk shipping service market, there are two main market types: time charter and voyage market. In a time charter agreement, a ship is leased for a set period at a fixed daily rate, regardless of the route or cargo carried. On the other hand, in the voyage market, ships are chartered for a single journey, with the rate typically based on the distance traveled and market conditions at the time of the charter. Each market type offers different advantages and risks for both shipowners and charterers.</p></p>
