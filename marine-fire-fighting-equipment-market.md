<p><p>The marine fire-fighting equipment market consists of two main types: Portable fire-fighting equipment and Fixed fire equipment. Portable fire-fighting equipment includes items such as fire extinguishers and hoses that can be easily moved and used in various locations on a ship. On the other hand, Fixed fire equipment refers to systems like sprinklers and fire alarms that are permanently installed in specific areas of a vessel to automatically detect and suppress fires. Both types of equipment are essential for ensuring the safety of crew members and protecting the ship from potential disasters.</p></p>
