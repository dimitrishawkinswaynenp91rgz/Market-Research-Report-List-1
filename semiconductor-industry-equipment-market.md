<p><p>The Semiconductor Industry Equipment market can be divided into three main types: Front Equipment, Subsequent Packaging, and Testing Equipment. Front Equipment includes tools for processing raw materials into semiconductor wafers. Subsequent Packaging Equipment involves tools for packaging the processed wafers into finished products such as integrated circuits. Testing Equipment is used to ensure the quality and functionality of the final semiconductor products before they are released to the market. These three types of equipment are essential for the production of semiconductors.</p></p>
