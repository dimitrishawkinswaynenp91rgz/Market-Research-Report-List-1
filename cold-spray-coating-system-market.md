<p><p>Cold spray coating system market is segmented into two types based on pressure: High Pressure Cold Spraying (HPCS) and Low-Pressure Cold Spraying (LPCS). HPCS involves the use of higher pressure to achieve coating deposition, resulting in improved coating quality and bond strength. On the other hand, LPCS utilizes lower pressure which may be more suitable for delicate substrates or materials. Both systems offer unique advantages and are utilized based on specific coating requirements and project needs within the industry.</p></p>
