<p><p>The Retail RFID Security Tags market can be divided into three main types based on frequency: HF (high frequency) tags, UHF (ultra-high frequency) tags, and LF (low frequency) tags. HF tags are typically used for item-level tracking in retail environments due to their short read range. UHF tags have a longer read range and are commonly used for inventory management. LF tags are often used for access control or tracking animals. Each type of tag has its own unique applications and benefits within the retail industry.</p></p>
