<p><p>Thermal Drying Type Silicone Ink and UV Curable Silicone Ink are two types of silicone inks used in the market. Thermal Drying Type Silicone Ink requires heat to dry and cure the ink on the substrate, while UV Curable Silicone Ink utilizes ultraviolet light to quickly cure the ink. Both types offer different advantages and applications in the printing industry. Thermal Drying Type Silicone Ink is preferred for its durability and resistance to chemicals, while UV Curable Silicone Ink is known for its fast curing time and eco-friendly characteristics.</p></p>
