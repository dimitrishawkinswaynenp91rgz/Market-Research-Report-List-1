<p><p>Automotive fuel injection refers to the method of delivering fuel into an engine's combustion chamber. There are three main types of fuel injection systems in the market. Port injection involves delivering fuel into the intake manifold before it enters the combustion chamber. Gasoline direct injection directly delivers fuel into the combustion chamber for gasoline engines. Diesel direct injection, on the other hand, directly injects fuel into the combustion chamber for diesel engines. These systems play a crucial role in ensuring efficient fuel delivery and combustion, resulting in improved engine performance and reduced emissions.</p></p>
