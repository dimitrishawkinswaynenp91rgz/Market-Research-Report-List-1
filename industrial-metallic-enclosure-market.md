<p><p>Industrial metallic enclosures come in two main types: wall-mounted and freestanding/floor-mounted. Wall-mounted enclosures are designed to be mounted directly onto a wall, saving floor space and offering easy access to equipment. Freestanding/floor-mounted enclosures, on the other hand, are standalone units that can be placed on the floor or mounted on a plinth. They offer more flexibility in terms of placement and can accommodate larger equipment or systems. Both types provide protection and security for sensitive electronic or mechanical components in industrial settings.</p></p>
