<p><p>Acrolein is produced through two different methods in the market: propylene oxidation method and glycerol dehydration method. In the propylene oxidation method, acrolein is derived from propylene through oxidation processes. On the other hand, the glycerol dehydration method involves converting glycerol into acrolein by eliminating water molecules. Both methods have their own advantages and applications in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, plastics, and chemical manufacturing.
