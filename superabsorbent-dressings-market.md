<p><ul><li>Non-Adhering Dressings</li><li>Self-Adhering Dressings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Superabsorbent dressings come in two main types: non-adhering dressings and self-adhering dressings. Non-adhering dressings have a layer that does not stick to the wound, making them gentle and easy to remove without causing further harm. Self-adhering dressings have a sticky backing that adheres to the skin and holds the dressing in place. Both types are designed to absorb excess fluids from wounds, promoting healing and preventing infections. The choice between the two depends on the individual's needs and preferences.</p></p>
