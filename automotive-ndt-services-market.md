<p><p>Automotive NDT services market includes various types of testing methods such as Ultrasonic Testing, Radiography Testing, Magnetic Particle Testing, Liquid Penetrant Testing, and Others. Ultrasonic Testing uses high-frequency sound waves to detect internal defects in materials. Radiography Testing utilizes X-rays or gamma rays to inspect the internal structure of components. Magnetic Particle Testing detects surface and near-surface flaws by applying magnetic field and particles. Liquid Penetrant Testing involves applying a liquid dye to detect surface defects. Other methods may include Visual Testing and Eddy Current Testing.</p></p>
