<p><ul><li>Semi-insulating</li><li>n-Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>4H Silicon Carbide Substrates come in two main market types - Semi-insulating and n-Type. Semi-insulating substrates have a high resistance and are ideal for high-power electronic devices such as RF power amplifiers and power switches. On the other hand, n-Type substrates have a lower resistance and are commonly used in high-frequency applications such as RF transistors and microwave devices. Both types offer excellent thermal conductivity, high breakdown voltage, and low power losses, making them essential materials in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
