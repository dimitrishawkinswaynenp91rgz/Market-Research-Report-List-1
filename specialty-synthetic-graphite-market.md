<p><p>Specialty synthetic graphite market is segmented into four main types based on production methods and properties: 1) Isotropic Graphite, which has consistent properties in all directions, making it ideal for high-performance applications. 2) Extruded Graphite, which is made through a forming process, resulting in high strength and density. 3) Molded Graphite, made using a compression molding process, offering good thermal conductivity and machinability. 4) Other types include specialty forms like flexible graphite sheets or foil, each tailored for specific applications within industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
