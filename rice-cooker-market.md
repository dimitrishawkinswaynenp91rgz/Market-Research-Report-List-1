<p><strong>The Rice Cooker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Rice Cooker</li><li>IH Rice Cooker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Traditional rice cookers are the most common type, using heating elements located under the inner pot to cook rice by boiling water and steam. On the other hand, IH rice cookers utilize electromagnetic induction technology to heat up the inner pot directly, resulting in more efficient and even cooking. IH rice cookers are also known for their precise temperature control and ability to cook different types of rice. Both types of rice cookers are popular in the market, catering to different preferences and cooking needs.</p></p>
